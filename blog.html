I'm excited to start writing more, though. I have lots of thoughts saved up to share.

I've written a lot of my thoughts in the same one notes page in the iOS Notes app ever since I've had an iPhone. Recently though, I bought a small notebook to write things down with a pen. I like taking out the item that represents what I'm doing when I do it. It's really tactile and enjoyable for reasons I don't know how to explain. Taking out a book to read, a notebook to write, a compact mirror to fix my makeup, whatever else. I'm trying to find more of these, I'll keep a list here. Let me know if you have any ideas.
